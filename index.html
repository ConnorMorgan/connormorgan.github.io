Intro

Hello there! I appreciate you checking out my portfolio site! I have a background in data science and business analytics, and I am currently studying for a Master's degree in Data Analytics. You can check out some of my certifications here. Check out my awesome projects.

Throughout my Master's degree, I have had a growing interest in customer retention, relations, product management, and utilizing machine learning algorithms to solve business problems such as forecasting, profit generation, and market analysis. For more about my current abilities look here, if you're interested continue reading to hear more about how I found a passion for data analytics.

I found a sense of control when I dove into analytics. While in college I quickly realized there is no one right way to do everything and it made me feel a little lost when figuring out what degree I wanted. I bounced around majors from business to biochemistry to computer science and finally found a huge passion in MIS (Still got a degree in 4 years too!). I grew a drive to improve and study optimally using methods to solve problems in my classes found from outside sources rather than the available textbooks, not Chegg... I thrive when I work quickly and efficiently, and I would focus on getting ahead to prevent wasting my time when workloads vary throughout the quarter. During this period of searching for a major I was truly passionate for I picked up coding and everything changed.

When I started to pick up coding while studying biochemistry, everything clicked. I could code out an entire computation heavy problem and have my computer do my math and erase human error from pressing tiny calculator buttons, and in comparing my work with the computer I created a homework checker on my own! I coded out labs, homework and study problems to tease my brain into learning how to code and practice new material efficiently in a very involved way which started a journey into computer science, MIS and now becoming a data analyst.
